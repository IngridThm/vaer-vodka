ABOUT

The recipe and method of our pure vodka has been perfected over generations. It is a cultural heritage from a bygone era where one had to be self-reliant with only the raw nature as a source of supply. Delivered from a hard bark fisherman to his urban grandson.

The name VAER derives from the plural local word vær. One calls a small fishing villages in the sea gap,without road connection, for fiskevær, the same word is also used about the weather and to be or exist as a human being.

COMMODITIES

We exclusively use organic potatoes of the local potato variety Gulløye, grown in a highly mineral-rich soil at the very end of the sea gap, where the waves strike ashore and the west wind blows in from the Barents Sea.

We use only fresh mineral water from an ancient artesian source from the local mountains. The water is rich in lime, minerals and salts, we consider this the most premium and purest water in the world.
